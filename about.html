About Dr. Vishal N Tyagi

ENT Specialist with 25+ years of experience

Professional Background

Dr. Vishal N Tyagi is a highly experienced ENT specialist with over 25 years of expertise in diagnosing and treating complex ENT conditions. He is known for a patient-first approach, combining advanced medical technology with compassionate care.

  • MS (ENT)
  • Minimally Invasive ENT Surgeries
  • Adult & Pediatric ENT Care
  • Hospital Attachments Across Mumbai

Professional Identity & Clinical Philosophy

The evolution of otolaryngology in India has transitioned from general outpatient care to highly specialized, technology-driven surgical practice. Within this landscape, Dr. Vishal Naresh Tyagi has established a professional identity that reflects precision, depth, and adaptability.

His approach to ENT care emphasizes comprehensive evaluation, evidence-based decision-making, and ethical patient communication. This philosophy enables continuity of care across pediatric and adult populations, particularly in complex urban healthcare systems.

Over the years, his work has consistently bridged the gap between advanced surgical technology and personalized patient-centric treatment.

Academic Foundation & Medical Training

Dr. Tyagi completed his foundational medical education at the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Medical Sciences (MGIMS), Sewagram — India’s first rural medical college, renowned for its rigorous clinical exposure and emphasis on socially responsible medicine.

He obtained his MBBS degree in 2001, followed by a Master of Surgery (MS) in ENT in 2008. His training during this period involved high-volume surgical exposure to ear, nose, throat, and head-neck pathologies.

  • MBBS – MGIMS, Sewagram (2001)
  • MS (ENT) – MGIMS, Sewagram (2008)
  • Maharashtra Medical Council Registration No. 24318

International Fellowships & Advanced Specialization

Recognizing the rapidly evolving nature of ENT and head-neck surgery, Dr. Tyagi pursued advanced international training to align his surgical practice with global benchmarks.

His fellowship training at the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (MSKCC), New York, provided exposure to advanced oncologic protocols, nerve-preservation techniques, and multidisciplinary tumor board decision-making.

Additional specialization includes cochlear implant surgery training in Innsbruck, Austria (MED-EL), and voice & laryngeal surgery training at the Kerala Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS), Trivandrum.

Comprehensive Surgical Expertise

Dr. Tyagi’s surgical practice encompasses advanced otology, rhinology, skull base surgery, head-neck oncology, thyroid surgery, and voice disorders. His procedural approach relies heavily on endoscopic and minimally invasive techniques to reduce morbidity and enhance recovery.

  • Cochlear Implantation & Hearing Restoration
  • Endoscopic Sinus & Skull Base Surgery
  • Thyroid, Salivary Gland & Head-Neck Oncology
  • Microlaryngeal & Phonosurgery

His ability to integrate multiple sub-specialties allows holistic management of complex ENT pathologies under a single continuum of care.

Institutional Practice & Infrastructure

Dr. Tyagi operates within a multi-institutional healthcare ecosystem across Mumbai and Thane. This allows him to deliver outpatient care, advanced diagnostics, and complex surgeries across tertiary hospitals equipped with modern infrastructure.

His practice is supported by advanced ENT workstations, 4K endoscopic systems, operating microscopes, nerve integrity monitors, and coblation technology — ensuring surgical precision and patient safety.

  • Fortis Hospital – Mulund
  • Holy Spirit Hospital – Andheri
  • Holy Family Hospital – Bandra
  • Infinity Medisurge – Thane

Academic Leadership & Research Contributions

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Tyagi has made significant contributions to medical education and research. He has served as Professor and Head of the ENT Department at SMBT Medical College, mentoring undergraduate and postgraduate students.

His academic output includes authorship of four ENT textbooks and peer-reviewed research publications, including studies on skull base diagnostics and surgical outcomes.

Participation in global collaborative studies such as COVIDSurg reflects his commitment to data-driven surgical safety and global knowledge exchange.

Patient-Centric Care & Long-Term Vision

Patients consistently describe Dr. Tyagi as approachable, meticulous, and transparent in communication. His emphasis on visual diagnostics and post-operative follow-up builds confidence and trust throughout the treatment journey.

His future vision integrates precision surgery, minimally invasive techniques, holistic wellness, and preventive care — ensuring outcomes that extend well beyond the operating room.
